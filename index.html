You're watching Bird-up!

It's time to duel! Lyrilusc Tri-brigade (Bird up!) is a fun and expressive deck for those that like combo decks. It can hold its own against the current top dogs, like Tearlaments.

Key cards that get you mistaken as a furry

Warbler: Ideal starter to have in your opening hand. This plus sparrow or wagtail means full combo! key part of why LL can save normal summon for other things.

Bird call: God-like spell that solves so many problems. Unclogs clunky hands, help you play through distruption, searches, Foolishes, does you taxes, gives you a massage, it does everything.

Ensemble Robin: Two BIG reasons to play this deck... Not once per turn, so can bounce as many times as it has material, some decks have no way of getting through this. Returns resources on pop, so you can still go off even if the opponent detroys this.

Tri-brigade nervall: The bridge between Lyrilusc and Tri-brigade. Nervall is a level 1 wind attribute winged beast, so it is searchable by sparrow and recital sterling. It is crucial for accessing tri-brigade monsters while comboing off with Lyrilusc.
